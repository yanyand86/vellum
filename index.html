Tips

Tidy handwriting. Switch the tray to Type and write on the page with your pencil. iPadOS Scribble converts it to text as you write, and Vellum sets it in your chosen handwriting font, sitting neatly on the lines. Make sure Scribble is on: Settings ▸ Apple Pencil ▸ Scribble.

Ink. The fountain pen, brush and calligraphy nibs respond to pressure; the pencil and pastel shade wider when you tilt, like real graphite. On newer iPads you will see a hover ring before the nib touches down.

Pencil gestures. Safari web apps receive pressure, tilt and hover, but Apple keeps squeeze, double-tap and barrel-roll for native apps — so those gestures won't change tools here.

Erasing. The eraser lifts whole strokes — brush over anything to remove it. Undo and redo live in the top bar.

Make it feel like an app. In Safari, tap Share ▸ Add to Home Screen for full-screen writing. Notes save on this device in the browser's storage.
